If a new vocabulary word is wanted, enter the filename in the input text box and speak the new vocabulary word after you submit the form. If you want to observe or delete a current vocabulary word, enter the file from the select list. Choose time or frequency domains to observe and enter the frequency domain parameters for frequency domain observation.

The filename should be the same as the new word you are adding to the vocabulary. It should end with the wav extension. The Audio Wav Directory must be specified for play/delete operations.





Frequency Domain Parameters

Enter frequency domain configuration. FFTs are averaged with 50% overlap. Zero-padding is used as necessary given the number of segments to average and the FFT size. The data is windowed to reduce sidelobe spectral leakage with a cost of main lobe widening and reduced resolution.
